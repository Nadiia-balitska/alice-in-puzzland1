About the game

"Alice Blast" is a dynamic match-3 puzzle game that transports players into a whimsical world inspired by Alice in Wonderland. With hundreds of levels, each offering unique puzzles and colorful animations, players match and blast their way through a fantastical landscape, unlocking magical power-ups and meeting classic characters.
